Goonzion Quartet

  • David Jaycock

Quality Jazz from Cornwall

Named after Goonzion downs - the small patch of moorland just outside the band's home of St Neot - Goonzion Quartet is made up of Paul Bullock - drums, Adam Taylor - bass, David Jaycock - guitar and Ed Hart - Trumpet. The band plays arrangements of standards from the 50s and 60s with a strong emphasis on improvisation including tunes by Bill Evans, Miles Davis, Wayne Shorter and Joe Henderson.
